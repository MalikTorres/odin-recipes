Sous Vide Steak

This is a page dedicated to Sous Vide steak

  • T Bone
  • Skirt
  • Rib Eye
  • Porterhouse
  1. First, a large container with water attach the Sous Vide
  2. Salt and pepper the steak and sit for 10-15 minutes
  3. Place steak into Sous Vide and let it Sous Vide
  4. Remove steak from the sous vide, discard liquid
